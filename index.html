About the Game

Game 1: Players will be shown a quick sequence of colors on the playboard. After a few seconds, the sequence disappears. The player must recall and reproduce the exact sequence. The sequence grows longer and faster each round until the player makes a mistake.

Game 2: Several lights glow randomly one after another. The player must press the correct switch immediately where the light glows. Missing a light, pressing wrongly, or delaying ends the game. The speed increases with every correct attempt.
