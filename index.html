About the Game

In Recall Rush there have basically two games :

1. Players will be shown a quick sequence of colors in few patterns on the playboard. After a few seconds, the sequence disappears. The player must quickly recall and reproduce the exact sequence in the correct order. With each round, the sequence becomes longer and faster, making it more challenging. The game continues until the player makes a mistake or runs out of time.

2. Several lights will glow one after another in random order. The player must press the button every time where a light glows. If the player misses even one light or presses at the wrong switch or delayed, they are disqualified. The game keeps getting faster, and the challenge is to survive as long as possible without mistakes.
